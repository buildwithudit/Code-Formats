Designing Dark Mode: It's Not Just #000000

Udit Sharma 4 Min Read

Many developers think Dark Mode is just setting background: black; color: white;. That is a rookie mistake. Pure black (#000000) creates high contrast that causes eye strain ("smearing") on OLED screens.

The Grey Scale Secret

Look at apps like Discord or VS Code. They don't use black. They use Dark Grey (e.g., #121212).

Why? Dark grey allows you to use shadows to show depth. On pure black, shadows are invisible, creating a flat look.
