3 Ways Developers Can Build Passive Income in 2025

Udit Sharma 6 Min Read

We developers have a superpower: we can create assets from thin air. While others have to buy real estate to earn passive income, we just need a code editor. Here is how I plan to monetize my skills.

1. Sell UI Templates

If you are good at Tailwind or React, stop building projects that sit in your GitHub "Graveyard." Polish them and put them on ThemeForest or Gumroad.

A simple "Admin Dashboard Template" can sell for $20. If 100 people buy it, that's $2000 for work you did once.
